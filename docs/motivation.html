Teleportation of humans and objects has been the subject of fascination for all of us for a very long time. One of the first examples of teleportation in literature was in an 1897 novel called To Venus in Five Seconds by Fred T. Jane.

In this tutorial, we wonât discuss this kind of teleportation but we will discuss Quantum Teleportation, which is a scheme for the transfer of quantum information, which is almost as cool as the teleportation of matter.

In 1993, Bennett et al.,:cite:`bennett1993teleporting` showed that quantum teleportation was actually possible using the rules of quantum mechanics, and has since been followed by experiments that demonstrate quantum teleportation.

Quantum Teleportation is not only cool, but also has important implications to quantum computing, quantum communication, and also interestingly to quantum hardware too :cite:`Pirandola_2015`.
